# Simon Game

A classic **Simon game** implemented using **HTML, CSS, and JavaScript**, with sound effects and a high score system saved in **localStorage**. This project demonstrates **DOM manipulation**, **event handling**, and **basic game logic** in the browser.

## Demo
*[Click here to play](https://talukderemran61.github.io/the-simon-game/)*  

---

## Features

- **Classic Simon Gameplay:** Repeat an increasing sequence of colors to score points.  
- **Keyboard & Mobile Support:** Start the game by pressing `A` on the keyboard or tapping the **Play** button on mobile devices.  
- **Sound Effects:** Each color has a unique sound; game-over and score-up sounds enhance the experience.  
- **Visual Feedback:** Buttons flash when clicked or during sequence playback. Background glows when a round is completed.  
- **Countdown Animation:** A 3-second countdown before the game starts.  
- **High Score Tracking:** Highest score is saved in **localStorage** and displayed on the UI.  
- **Responsive Design:** Optimized for both desktop and mobile screens.  

---

## How to Play

1. **Start the Game**:  
   - Press **A** on your keyboard (desktop)  
   - Or tap the **Play** button (mobile)  

2. **Follow the Sequence**:  
   Watch the colors light up and try to repeat the sequence by clicking the buttons.  

3. **Increase Your Score**:  
   Each round completed correctly increases your score by 1.  

4. **Game Over**:  
   If you press the wrong color, the game ends. Your highest score is saved automatically.
